Process

Here's how I approach responsive website design :

  1. Consultation & Planning : Start by discussing your business goals, target audience, and project requirements to form a solid foundation.
  2. Design Prototyping : I create wireframes and mockups to visually demonstrate how your website will look and function across devices.
  3. Development : I build the website using modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, incorporating best practices for responsiveness.
  4. Testing : Rigorous testing is performed across multiple devices and browsers to ensure flawless performance.
  5. Launch & Support : After final revisions, your website goes live! Ongoing support is available to ensure smooth operation post-launch.
